## Summary of the impact

One paragraph (REF indicative limit ~100 words) stating the impact:
what changed beyond academia, for whom, and the reach and significance
of that change.

## Underpinning research

Summarise the key findings and the research that produced them — usually
2–6 outputs from this Unit of Assessment, published 2021–2028. Note who
carried it out and when.

## Details of the impact

Narrate the impact: the pathway from the research to the effect, the
beneficiaries, and the evidence of reach and significance. Refer to the
corroborating sources listed below.

## Sources to corroborate

- A stakeholder testimonial (name / role / organisation)
- A policy document, report or URL
- Usage figures, press coverage, or other independent evidence
